    \section{\texorpdfstring{\textbf{How many visits does it take to make
your pass worth
it?}}{How many visits does it take to make your pass worth it?}}\label{how-many-visits-does-it-take-to-make-your-pass-worth-it}

\textbf{TL;DR} - \textbf{\emph{SoCal Select (cheapest)}:} pay \$430 for
the pass + parking and go 3 times (\$561 if you paid every time) -
\textbf{\emph{Deluxe}:} pay \$830 for the pass + parking and go 5 times
(\$935 if you paid every time) - \textbf{\emph{Signature}:} pay \$1000
for the pass, parking comes as a benefit with the pass, and go 6 times
(\$1122 if you paid every time) - \textbf{\emph{Signature Plus}:} pay
\$1150 for the pass, parking comes as a benefit, and go 7 times (\$1309
if you paid every time)

\textbf{NOTE:} - Parking at Disneyland is \$20 - ALWAYS consider
blockout dates. This is mentioned below in the results section 2.1 -
Disney has enabled a NEW POLICY JUNE 1ST, 2018 that creates differing
blockout dates for both parks.

\subsection{Assumptions}\label{assumptions}

For every time a guest visits, that guest will: - Enjoy their visit
(meaning the individual ticket price was worth it) - Go to both parks -
Pay a regular priced ticket - NOTE: Parking is not considered (yet)

\textbf{Base Price of a Park Hopper ticket (Regular not Peak):} \$167

\begin{definition}
You've made your "money's worth" when:
$$
(n \times b) > (AP)
$$
Where:
- __n__: number of visits
- __b__: base price (generally \$97 - \$135 Disney alone, \$147 - \$187 Parkhopper)
- __AP__: Annual Passport price
\end{definition}

\subsection{In-Depth}\label{in-depth}

Although the SoCal Select is the easiest pass to meet its worth, all
weekends and most major holidays (including the previous and following
5-10 day time span) are blocked out. If you are a student/parent, simply
being able to go for a whole day can be difficult.

The Deluxe is fairly reasonable as it does allow for several weekend
visits and significantly less blockout dates. The biggest drawback is
the jump in price and still does not include free parking, so each visit
will have an automatic \$20 payment, same goes for SoCal Select
(assuming the guest parks in the parking structure)

A \emph{Signature} and \emph{Signature Plus} Passport meet their worth
largely due to parking, reduction in blockout dates, and higher
discounts. While \emph{SoCal} and \emph{Deluxe} offer 10\% off on food
and merchandise, \emph{Signature} and \emph{Signature Plus} offer 15\%
off food and 20\% off on merchandise. The worth of that is determined by
the guest.

A \emph{Signature Plus} is also the only pass to have 0 blockout dates
and offers Disney's \emph{MaxPass}. This allows for:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  FastPass selection on mobile devices
\item
  Disney PhotoPass downloads
\end{itemize}

To buy the MaxPass as an addition to a passport is \$75. For every
individual ticket, it would cost \$10 per ticket.
